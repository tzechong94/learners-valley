Frequently Asked Questions

Drill and practice, which is what most tuition centres offer, is not the only way to master concepts and ace examinations. This method may be effective for improving scores, but its effect is temporary and it prohibits students from achieving a deep level of understanding. Thus, students often find themselves having difficulty with applying their knowledge and skills to new scenarios. This method also makes learning mundane and limits students from developing problem-solving skills.

At Learners Valley, our aim is to ignite a child’s natural curiosity to learn. We want our students to see the purpose in their learning and to be motivated to do well in all aspects of life. We achieve this by making the learning process fun and engaging for them, and by helping them to develop the desired skill sets and dispositions for life-long learning. When students find meaning in their learning and become interested in learning, they will naturally improve and do well in their examinations.

Each and every lesson at Learners Valley is carefully curated by our curriculum specialists - experienced former MOE teachers who are familiar with the syllabus and the examination standards, and know exactly how to teach to help your child learn best. Your child will go through an enjoyable and fruitful 1.5-hour lesson, which will help to establish the foundation, achieve a deep level of understanding and develop skills for application of knowledge. Your child will also receive learning materials that are specially crafted to help him or her master the syllabus content fully. All our lesson plans and materials are also being reviewed on a regular basis for fine-tuning and to address the ever-changing assessment trends.

Learners Valley specialises in the teaching of English and Mathematics at the Primary level according to MOE syllabus and examination standards. We offer tuition classes for these two subjects for Primary 3 to Primary 6 students. Each lesson lasts 1.5 hours.

As educators, we know the benefits of a low teacher-student ratio - enhanced engagement, individualised attention, personalised feedback and many more! Therefore, we will only enrol a maximum of 8 students in a class.
