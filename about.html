About Us

Learners Valley was founded by a former MOE teacher and her co-founder who have aced the education system in Singapore. From our own experiences in top schools in Singapore, we are determined to help children excel in the education system and to encourage them to pursue their passions. We aim to nurture children to be curious, creative and determined to try their best in whatever they do. We do that through empowering children with problem-solving skills, exposing children to new and meaningful experiences so that they will never lose their sense of wonder, and inculcating grit in children.

What we believe about teaching and learning

Keeping Children Curious

We believe that every child has a curious mind and the ability to learn. Learning should be kept fun, challenging, motivating and satisfying.

Empowering kids with values

We believe that every child should take ownership of their own learning in whatever skills that they want to excel in, through discipline and consistency. Our role is to facilitate their learning process and to help them adopt a positive attitude towards life.

Exploration and Discovery

We believe that children should be encouraged to explore, to take risks, and to learn from their mistakes so as to achieve their learning and discover their true self.
