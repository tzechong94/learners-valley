This was why we began.

Learners Valley was founded by two secondary school mates who share a common vision for teaching and learning. Upon completing their secondary education with flying colours, one underwent rigorous training to become an MOE teacher while the other embarked on his journey as a private tutor. As high achievers of a top secondary school in Singapore, the founders know exactly how best to learn and thrive in local schools. As teachers with years of experience, they know exactly how to facilitate the learning process to maximise learning. They empathise with the children who struggle to keep up with the pace in school and understand the yearn in fast learners to be challenged. As teachers, they wished that the bulk of their time could be invested in lesson planning rather than administrative work.

This is why Learners Valley is established. We are here to fulfil each child's individual learning needs and unleash his or her potential. We are determined to help our students excel in school and to guide them in pursuing their passions. We strive to ignite their innate sense of curiosity, creativity and determination. We encourage them to always put their best foot forward in whatever they do. We achieve these through empowering them with problem-solving skills and providing them with meaningful learning experiences. We want the children in our hands to have grit and to never lose their sense of wonder.

Our Educational Beliefs

Keeping Children Curious

We believe that every child has a curious mind and the ability to learn. Learning should be kept fun, challenging, motivating and satisfying.

Empowering kids with values

We believe that every child should take ownership of their own learning in whatever skills that they want to excel in, through discipline and consistency. Our role is to facilitate their learning process and to help them adopt a positive attitude towards life.

Exploration and Discovery

We believe that children should be encouraged to explore, to take risks, and to learn from their mistakes so as to achieve their learning and discover their true self.
