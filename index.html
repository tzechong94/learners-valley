About Us

Learners Valley was founded by a former MOE teacher and an experienced private tutor. With our expert knowledge and skills in teaching, and experiences as high achievers of a top school in Singapore, we are determined to help the children of today to excel in life. We aim to nurture children’s sense of curiosity, creativity and determination through empowering them with problem-solving skills in the learning process. We want the children of Learners Valley to have grit and to preserve their sense of wonder.

What To Expect at Learners Valley

Proven Pedagogy for Concept Mastery

Our curriculum specialists are experienced former MOE teachers who are familiar with the syllabus and examination standards. The design of our lessons is backed by learning theories and teaching experiences in an actual classroom setting. Our tutors use only effective instructional strategies to help our students master concepts. They are also able to adapt instructions and learning materials accordingly to meet the individual learning needs of our students.

Formative Assessment, Quality Feedback and Targeted Remediation

Besides using the right methods to teach, we closely monitor our students' progress through formative assessment. The data collected from the assessment enables our tutors to address misconceptions timely. It also helps our tutors to identify each student's learning gaps and intervene promptly before they accumulate. Personalised quality feedback will be given to our students based on the work done to guide them to improve.

After-class Support

Learning continues beyond the classroom. At Learners Valley, we strive to provide our students with our fullest support. Our tutors are here to answer any questions that our students have at any point in time. An online portal with plentiful learning resources is also in the pipeline for our students to revise independently at their own time and pace.
