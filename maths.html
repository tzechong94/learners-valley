Learners Valley Primary Maths Programme

Help your child reach their fullest potential

Learn Maths Concepts Better

Our Primary School Maths Programme is crafted by in-house curriculum experts to ensure that it is progressive and effective for our learners. We focus on explaining the first principles of these concepts, and helping children to understand the concepts and not memorise methods. We also teach children how to apply these concepts to new problems that they see, so they will then learn how to use what they have learned in their tests, and most importantly, in their daily lives.

Our Approach to Primary School Maths

Readiness

In this first phase of introducing any new topic, our Maths specialists will help students recall basic Maths facts to check their factual fluency. Often, new topics are built upon previous concepts, and therefore it is important for children to see the link between what they have learned and what they are about to learn. We activate prior knowledge required for deepening, in alignment to MOE's curriculum, and help children revise previous knowledge through examples and practice before moving into new Maths topics. This helps to close any knowledge gaps that they might have, and allows for faster mastery of new Maths concepts.

Engagement

Instead of just teaching, we aim to help children acquire new Mathematical concepts through activities and scaffolding. Our Maths specialists keep students engaged by guiding and challenging them as they work through understanding new Maths concepts. We do not believe in providing answers for children. We engage and challenge children while guiding them, in order to help them feel fulfilled when they work through problems on their own successfully. This way, they build the capabilities to use new concepts that they learned, and also build resilience when solving problems on their own.

Mastery

Our goal is to help children master these concepts so that it becomes second nature to them. We have formative assessment to check on their learning and to help close any learning gaps immediately so they do not accumulate. To help children master these Primary School Maths topic, our Primary Maths tuition programme consists of practice questions with varied levels of difficulty, and we get children to solve real world problems with these new skills that they acquire.
