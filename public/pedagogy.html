Our Pedagogy

Our lessons are carefully designed and structured based on learning theory (Gagne’s Nine Events of Instruction) to help our students achieve the desired learning outcomes. Coupled with our 5 key instructional strategies, we promise our students a fun, engaging, meaningful and effective learning.

Key Instructional Strategies

Inquiry-based learning

  • We do not acquire knowledge by memorising or relying on rote learning.
  • Knowledge and skills are best acquired and retained when we see how they can be applied in our everyday life.
  • At Learners Valley, we give our students real life authentic contexts to help them understand and apply the new concept and skill.

Diagnosis and Formative Assessment

  • Is your child ready to learn a new topic?
  • Does he/she have the prerequisite knowledge required to learn the new skill or concept?
  • A student struggles to learn the new concept not because it is difficult but because he has yet to get the basics right.
  • At Learners Valley, students will do a diagnostic test at the start of each new topic. This enables the teacher to assess whether the student is ready to move forward and whether a recall of prior knowledge is necessary before introducing the new concept.

Feedback and Remediation

  • I have done my work. So, what’s next?
  • There is no meaning in doing worksheets after worksheets when you are not going to revisit and improve on them. At Learners Valley, quality feedback will be given to the student to help him/her identify his/her areas of strengths and weaknesses. With this information, we help to build the student’s sense of confidence and identify his weaknesses for targeted remediation to close the learning gap.
  • Make every piece of work done purposeful and meaningful!

E-pedagogy

  • The traditional lecture approach to teaching is out. In our modern society, technology is here to transform teaching and learning. It supports collaborative learning, visualisation of information and enables teachers to provide real-time feedback.
  • At Learners Valley, we integrate technology into our classroom to engage our students and deepen their learning. We want to encourage more active participation and to cater to the different learning needs of our students with the endless possibilities supported by technology.

Differentiated Instruction

  • Every child is unique. No two students learn and grow in the same way and at the same pace.
  • At Learners Valley, we acknowledge this fact and make use of differentiated instruction in our classroom of diverse learners to help each and every child to develop to their full potential in the way that is best suited for him/her.
  • We tailor our teaching strategies and materials to meet each child’s unique interests and needs. It is about customising the classroom for you!
