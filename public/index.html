About Us

Learners Valley was founded by a former MOE teacher and her co-founder who have aced the education system in Singapore. From our own experiences in top schools in Singapore, we are determined to help children excel in the education system and to encourage them to pursue their passions. We aim to nurture children to be curious, creative and determined to try their best in whatever they do. We do that through empowering children with problem-solving skills, exposing children to new and meaningful experiences so that they will never lose their sense of wonder, and inculcating grit in children.
